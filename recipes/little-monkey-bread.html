Author: Gracie Gordon Prep Time: 5 Cook Time: 25 Total Time: 30 minutes Yield: 1 1x Category: Dessert Method: Bake Cuisine: American Diet: Vegetarian Ingredients Scale 1x 2x 3x 3 pieces Pillsbury Crescent Rolls dough 1 teaspoon granulated sugar 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon 1 teaspoon brown sugar 1/2 tablespoon butter, melted Cook Mode Prevent your screen from going dark Instructions Preheat oven to 350 degrees Grease an oven-safe bowl or ramekin with cooking spray or butter Cut each piece of crescent roll dough into 4 pieces and roll into balls In a small bowl combine granulated sugar and cinnamon. Roll each piece of dough in the mixture and place in ramekin Stir brown sugar with butter and pour over the dough Bake for 20 minutes https://hungry-blonde.com/single-serve-monkey-bread-simple-dessert-for-one/
